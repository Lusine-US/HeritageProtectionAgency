We are the voice of all who believe that cultural heritage is vital for our economy, our society, our culture, our environment, our well-being and for the future of Europe. Cultural Heritage is on loan from future generations and good stewardship in the present is, therefore, the responsibility of everyone living in Europe. To put culture and cultural heritage at the very core of the European project on behalf of our members as the largest representative civil society movement in Europe to the advantage of all Europeans.

UNESCO

HEADQUARTERS - France Place de Fontenay, Paris.This organization founded in 04,November 1946 Mission of UNESCO is to develop educational tools for everyone,free of hate,free of intolerance,free of racism and free of orientation.Equal dignity of all cultures, including heritage sites under threat, danger and vandalism. The main objective as an organization is to stand with a global protection of human rights, social justice, to promote peace around the world. The list of World Heritage in danger has been created to Identify,Protect and Conserve all the heritage sites around the world.

Europa Nostra

Europa Nostra was founded on 29 November 1963 in Paris. For over 50 years, they’ve been protecting and lobbying cultural and natural heritage sites.The Europa Nostra is a Non-Profit, Heritage Organization operating in Europe. They are tasked with protecting Europe’s cultural and natural heritage.Our mission is to give shape and voice to an ever-growing citizens’ movement to support cultural and natural heritage across Europe.
